## 📌 Problem Statement

Many organizations struggle with ESG reporting due to disconnected systems, manual data entry, and lack of real-time visibility. While ERP systems collect operational data, ESG performance monitoring often remains fragmented and reactive.

**EcoSphere** integrates ESG directly into day-to-day ERP operations by:
- Automating carbon emissions calculation from operational transactions
- Tracking CSR activities and employee participation
- Monitoring governance compliance and policy acknowledgements
- Encouraging sustainability through gamification

---

## 💡 Solution

EcoSphere is a comprehensive ESG Management Platform built on Odoo that transforms sustainability management from a manual, disconnected process into an integrated, real-time, and engaging experience.

The platform consists of four core modules that work together seamlessly:

- **Environmental Module**: Automates carbon accounting using configurable emission factors linked to ERP operations (Purchases, Manufacturing, Expenses, Fleet). Tracks department-level emissions and sustainability goals.

- **Social Module**: Manages CSR activities, tracks employee participation with proof-based approvals, monitors diversity metrics, and measures training completion.

- **Governance Module**: Handles ESG policies, policy acknowledgements, audit management, and compliance issue tracking with automated alerts for overdue issues.

- **Gamification Module**: Engages employees through challenges, badges, XP points, rewards, and leaderboards - making sustainability participation fun and rewarding.

The platform provides unified dashboards and customizable reports, enabling management to make data-driven decisions with real-time ESG insights.

# ✨ Features

- ✅ **Dashboard & Analytics** - Unified ESG dashboard with department and organization-wide scores
- ✅ **Carbon Emissions Tracking** - Automated calculation from ERP operations with configurable emission factors
- ✅ **CSR Activity Management** - Track employee participation with proof-based approvals
- ✅ **Governance Compliance** - Policy management, audits, and compliance issue tracking
- ✅ **Gamification Engine** - Challenges, XP, badges, rewards, and leaderboards
- ✅ **Department Scorecards** - Individual ESG scores per department with customizable weighting
- ✅ **Report Generation** - Environmental, Social, Governance, and custom reports with PDF/Excel/CSV export
- ✅ **Notification System** - In-app and email alerts for approvals, reminders, and issue alerts
- ✅ **Settings & Administration** - Configurable business rules, categories, and notification preferences
- ✅ **Responsive UI** - Mobile-friendly interface for on-the-go access
- ✅ **Role-Based Access** - Secure access control for different user types
